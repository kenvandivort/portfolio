Hello World

I am Ken Vandivort. I enjoy good coffee, music, and dogs. I grew up in Tulsa and graduated from Oklahoma State University with a B.A. in Sociology. I moved to Austin in 2005 and have dedicated the past ten years of work to helping people help themselves get clean and sober. At home in my freetime, I started learning web design online in 2016. Then, I started attending Austin Coding Academy in 2017 to take my knowledge to a professional level. I am excited to see what the future holds in my new web designer career.

Technical Skills

User Experience design, wireframes, responsive design, Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Flexbox, Git/Github, Javascript, JQuery, Google Analytics, SEO.

Human Skills

I am known to be calm in crisis, empathetic, and high EQ. Patience is one of my best virtues. I am certified in first-aid, CPR, and non-violent crisis intervention.

Extra Skills

Aquired extranious music knowledge. I don't play drums as much as I used to and am a bit of a jack of all trades. I play well with dogs and humans.
